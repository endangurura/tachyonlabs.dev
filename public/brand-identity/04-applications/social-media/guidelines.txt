Social Media Asset Guidelines
=============================

Profile Pictures:
- Use icon-only version
- Ensure adequate padding (20% minimum)
- Black on light backgrounds
- White on dark backgrounds

Cover Images:
- Keep important content in safe area
- YouTube: Center 1546×423px is always visible
- Use horizontal logo with tagline
- Maintain brand consistency

Post Templates:
- Include logo in top-left corner
- Use grid system for alignment
- Leave space for content
- Maintain visual hierarchy

Color Usage:
- Twitter/X: Black or white based on theme
- LinkedIn: Professional (black on white)
- GitHub: High contrast for visibility
- Instagram: Adapt to content style

File Naming:
[platform]-[type]-[color].png

Included Assets:
- Profile pictures (400×400, 500×500)
- Cover images (platform-specific sizes)
- Post templates (1200×1200, 1080×1080)
- Color variants (black, white, neutral)
