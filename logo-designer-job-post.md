# Logo Designer Needed for AI Research Lab - Tachyon Labs

## About the Project

We are seeking an experienced logo designer to create a distinctive and meaningful brand identity for **Tachyon Labs**, an AI research organization focused on advancing artificial intelligence with scientific rigor and safety. Our logo should embody trust, innovation, and our commitment to responsible AI development.

## Design Inspiration

We admire the sophisticated simplicity and professionalism of these AI industry leaders:
- [Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/) / [Claude](https://claude.ai/) - Clean, trustworthy aesthetic
- [Cohere](https://cohere.com/) - Modern, technical sophistication
- [Cognition](https://cognition.ai/) - Bold yet refined
- [Cursor](https://www.cursor.com/) - Minimalist, developer-focused
- [Grok](https://grok.com/) - Playful yet professional

## Project Scope & Deliverables

### Initial Phase
- **3-4 unique concept designs** with rationale for each approach
- Presentation of concepts in context (mockups)
- Initial color palette recommendations

### Refinement Phase
- **2 rounds of revisions** on selected concept
- Exploration of variations and refinements
- Typography pairing recommendations

### Final Deliverables
1. **Complete Logo Package**
   - Master logo files (AI, EPS, SVG)
   - Web-ready formats (PNG, SVG, ICO)
   - High-resolution formats (JPG, PNG at various sizes)
   - Animated version (optional bonus)

2. **Logo Variations**
   - Full color version
   - Monochrome/single color version
   - Reversed versions (for dark backgrounds)
   - Minimal/icon version (for favicons, app icons)
   - Horizontal and vertical lockups

3. **Brand Guidelines Document**
   - Color specifications (HEX, RGB, CMYK, Pantone)
   - Typography guidelines
   - Minimum size requirements
   - Clear space requirements
   - Usage do's and don'ts
   - Application examples

## Design Requirements

### Visual Style
- **Modern & Professional**: Clean lines, thoughtful use of negative space
- **Scientifically Precise**: Conveys accuracy and attention to detail
- **Trustworthy**: Inspires confidence in our AI safety mission
- **Scalable**: Works perfectly from 16px favicon to billboard size

### Technical Requirements
- Vector-based design (no raster elements in core logo)
- Must work in both RGB and CMYK color spaces
- Accessible color contrast ratios
- Optimized for digital-first applications

### Conceptual Considerations
- Should subtly reference speed/acceleration (Tachyon = faster-than-light particle)
- Balance technical sophistication with approachability
- Avoid clich√©d AI imagery (brain circuits, robot faces, etc.)
- Consider abstract or geometric approaches
