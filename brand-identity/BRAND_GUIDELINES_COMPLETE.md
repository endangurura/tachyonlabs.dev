## Logo System

### The Quantum Loop
Our logo symbolizes continuous innovation through an incomplete circle with a ghost path, representing unexplored potential and ongoing progress toward ASI.

#### Logo Construction
- **Grid**: Built on a 13×13 unit system
- **Proportions**: 26×26 units for icon, 180×26 for full logo
- **Spacing**: 6 units between icon and wordmark
- **Clear Space**: Minimum 1× icon height on all sides

#### Logo Anatomy
1. **Main Path**: 270° arc representing progress
2. **Ghost Path**: 90° arc at 30% opacity showing potential
3. **Center Dot**: Focus point representing convergence
4. **Wordmark**: Mona Sans, 600 weight, -0.02em tracking

### Logo Variations

#### Primary Configurations
1. **Horizontal Logo** (Default)
   - Use for: Headers, business cards, general applications
   - Minimum size: 120px digital, 30mm print

2. **Stacked Logo**
   - Use for: Square formats, social media
   - Minimum size: 80px digital, 20mm print

3. **Icon Only**
   - Use for: App icons, favicons, profile pictures
   - Minimum size: 16px digital, 5mm print

#### Color Variations
- **Black**: For light backgrounds (#F5F5F5 or lighter)
- **White**: For dark backgrounds (#737373 or darker)
- **Do not** create additional color variations

### Incorrect Usage
❌ Do not rotate or tilt the logo
❌ Do not stretch or compress
❌ Do not change colors
❌ Do not add effects (shadows, gradients)
❌ Do not separate elements
❌ Do not recreate the logo

## Typography

### Primary Typeface: Mona Sans
A modern geometric sans-serif with variable width capabilities, representing precision and innovation.

#### Font Stack
```
font-family: 'Mona Sans', -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif;
```

### Type Scale

| Style | Size | Weight | Line Height | Tracking | Usage |
|-------|------|--------|-------------|----------|--------|
| Display | 72px/4.5rem | 600 | 1.1 | -0.03em | Hero headlines |
| H1 | 48px/3rem | 600 | 1.2 | -0.02em | Page titles |
| H2 | 36px/2.25rem | 600 | 1.25 | -0.02em | Section headers |
| H3 | 24px/1.5rem | 600 | 1.35 | -0.01em | Subsections |
| H4 | 20px/1.25rem | 500 | 1.4 | 0 | Card titles |
| Body | 16px/1rem | 400 | 1.75 | 0 | Body text |
| Small | 14px/0.875rem | 400 | 1.5 | 0 | Captions |
| Micro | 12px/0.75rem | 500 | 1.35 | 0.01em | Labels |

### Typography Guidelines
- **Hierarchy**: Maintain clear visual hierarchy
- **Contrast**: Minimum 4.5:1 for body text, 3:1 for large text
- **Line Length**: 45-75 characters for optimal readability
- **Spacing**: Use consistent spacing scale (4, 8, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64)

## Applications

### Business Cards
- **Size**: 3.5" × 2" (US Standard)
- **Orientation**: Horizontal
- **Logo Size**: 15mm height
- **Typography**: Name 18pt, Title 14pt, Contact 11pt
- **Variants**: Minimal (white) and Premium (black)

### Letterhead
- **Size**: 8.5" × 11" (US Letter)
- **Margins**: 0.75" all sides
- **Logo Size**: 20mm height
- **Header**: Logo + company name + tagline
- **Footer**: 3-column contact information

### Email Signatures
```html
[Logo - 30px height]
Name Surname
Title | Tachyon Labs
email@tachyonlabs.dev | tachyonlabs.dev
```

### Presentation Templates
- **Logo Position**: Top left, 20px margins
- **Title Slides**: Logo + centered content
- **Content Slides**: Small logo, consistent headers
- **Typography**: System fonts for compatibility

## Print Guidelines

### General Specifications
- **Color Mode**: CMYK for 4-color process
- **Black**: 100% K for text, rich black for large areas
- **Resolution**: 300 DPI minimum
- **Bleed**: 0.125" (3mm) standard

### Paper Recommendations
- **Business Cards**: 16pt premium matte
- **Letterhead**: 70lb uncoated text
- **Brochures**: 100lb silk cover

### Special Finishes
- **Spot UV**: Logo only on dark backgrounds
- **Foil Stamp**: Silver or white on premium applications
- **Emboss/Deboss**: Minimum 10mm logo size
